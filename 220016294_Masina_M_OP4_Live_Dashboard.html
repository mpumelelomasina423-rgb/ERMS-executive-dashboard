SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals
ERMS monitoring converts internal governance into nationally reportable development progress. The VUCA disruption has strengthened the case for each SDG 17 linkage — records resilience is inseparable from accountability (United Nations, 2015).
My Opinion — These Linkages Are Causal, Not Aspirational
I argue that the ERMS–SDG 17 relationship is not a box-ticking exercise. A metadata integrity failure would directly invalidate South Africa's legal exchange commitments (17.17). A 9% audit trail gap is a measurable failure of NDP 2030 reporting obligations (17.18). And failure to reach Level 3 maturity will undermine the Steering Committee's credibility with NARSSA and the State Security Agency (17.16). The VUCA incident proved that records governance is the government's primary accountability instrument — not a supporting function.
